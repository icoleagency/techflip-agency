COMMON QUESTIONS

FAQ

Do I need any experience in reselling or tech? +
Zero experience needed. We handle all the technical work — sourcing, testing, listing, shipping. You just invest and track your returns through your dashboard. We built this so anyone can participate in the pre-owned tech market.
How do I know my money is being used properly? +
Every client gets a custom CRM dashboard with real-time visibility into their inventory, active listings, and sales. You can log in any time and see exactly which devices are listed, which have sold, and what profit was generated. A formal partnership agreement is signed before any funds are transferred.
When do I start seeing returns? +
After your investment is received and the partnership agreement is signed, we source and list your first batch of devices within 7–10 business days. Most clients see their first sales within the first 2 weeks. Monthly profit distributions begin at the end of your first full month.
What happens if a device doesn't sell? +
Pre-owned Apple and Samsung devices have extremely high demand — our average sell time is 3–7 days. In the rare case a device takes longer, we adjust pricing strategically to move it. Your capital is always actively working.
Can I upgrade my tier later? +
Absolutely. Many clients start at the Starter tier and reinvest their profits to move up to Growth or Elite. We make it easy to scale — just let your account manager know and we'll adjust your inventory allocation accordingly.
Are the devices verified and legitimate? +
Every single device goes through IMEI verification, blacklist checking, and full functionality testing before it's listed. We only sell unlocked, clean devices — no blacklisted, carrier-locked, or iCloud-locked devices ever make it into your inventory.
What is the projected return on my investment? +
Returns vary based on tier and market conditions, but our clients typically see 20–30% monthly ROI on their managed inventory. Starter clients average $800–$1,500/month. Growth clients average $1,500–$2,800/month. Elite clients average $3,500–$6,000+/month.
